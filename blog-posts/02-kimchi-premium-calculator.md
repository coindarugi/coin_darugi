# Kimchi Premium Calculator: Track Korean Crypto Exchange Arbitrage Opportunities

## What is Kimchi Premium?

The **Kimchi Premium** refers to the price difference between cryptocurrency prices on Korean exchanges (Upbit, Bithumb, Coinone) and international exchanges (Coinbase, Binance). This phenomenon is named after kimchi, Korea's famous fermented vegetable dish.

## Understanding Kimchi Premium

### Why Does It Exist?

1. **Capital Controls**: Strict Korean financial regulations
2. **High Demand**: Strong retail crypto interest in Korea
3. **Limited Arbitrage**: Difficulty moving funds internationally
4. **Local Market Dynamics**: Korean investor sentiment

### Historical Context

- **2017 Bull Run**: Kimchi premium reached 30-50%
- **2021 Rally**: Premium spiked to 20-25%
- **2024-2025**: Typically ranges from -5% to +15%
